A roguelike tower defense game.
Survive for 50 waves without losing your core. Choose a reward, improve your build, survive the next wave, repeat. Highly replayable, difficult, and addictive. You'll want just one more wave, just one more run!

Quick Facts

  • Release on itch.io: January 16, 2020 (Early Access)

  • Developed by Berlin-based solo indie dev ehmprah
